Scale Game - Find the Heavy Coin

How to Play

One of the coins is heavier than the others (but they look identical). Your goal is to find the heavy coin by weighing them on the scale.

Drag coins to either side of the scale, then click "Weigh" to see which side is heavier or if they're balanced.

Once you think you've found the heavy coin, drag it to the basket below for verification!

Use the controls above to change the number of coins and reset the game.
